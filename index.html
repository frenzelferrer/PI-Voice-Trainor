How to Use

Recite Mode: Click the microphone and speak digits one by one. The app validates each digit in real-time. Say "hint" for help.

Listen Mode: Hear the digits read aloud at your chosen speed. Follow along visually as each digit is highlighted.

Quiz Mode: Answer 10 random-position questions to test your memory of specific digits.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • Space — Toggle listening
  • H — Get a hint
  • R — Read next 10 digits
  • Esc — End session
